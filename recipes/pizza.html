Classic Cheese Pizza

Simple and Delicious Cheese Pizza! Flaky, Soft Crust.

Fast and easy cheese pizza recipe for any occasion. Guaranteed to be soft and delicious whenever the craving hits.Sometimes all you want at the end of the day is a simple cheese pizza. This recipe turns simple into sublime with the addition of an exceptional pizza dough, low-moisture mozzarella cheese, and an easy to make tomato sauce that hits all of the right sweet and savory notes to marry all of the flavors in this pie. A simple garnish of fresh herbs, and you've got perfection on a plate.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Ball of Pizza Dough
  • Flour
  • 1/4 Cup Pizza Sauce
  • 3 ounces of Mozarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Let chilled dough stand at room tempurature until cool.
  2. Step 2: Preheat oven and pan to 500*F for 30 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Stretch dough to a 10 inch circle of even thickness.
  4. Step 4: Add sauce and cheese to desired amounts.
  5. Step 5: Put pizza on preheated pan and into the oven.
